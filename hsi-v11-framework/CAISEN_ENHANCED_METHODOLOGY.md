## Core Philosophy

### 蔡森's Four Golden Rules

1. **量在價先 (Volume Precedes Price)**
   - Volume is THE leading indicator
   - Money flow determines price, not lagging indicators
   - "股票交易就是量跟價，金錢決定股票的價格，是最當下、最即時的訊號"
   - MACD, KD, RSI are all delayed and less meaningful

2. **小賠大賺 (Small Losses, Big Gains)**
   - Cut losses quickly, let profits run
   - Risk/reward ratio must favor gains (minimum 1:2)
   - "支撐就是小幅度小機率失敗的停損風險，滿足就是在有限的風險下，大機率獲取更大的利潤"

3. **嚴守停損 (Strict Stop-Loss)**
   - Never violate your stop-loss
   - Survival is more important than being right
   - Set stops based on pattern structure, not arbitrary percentages

4. **隨勢而為 (Follow the Trend)**
   - Trade with the trend, not against it
   - "破底翻" success rate: 70-80% in bull market, only 50% in bear/sideways market
   - The trend is your friend

### Key Quote from 蔡森

> "底部成型，不是均線可以看得出來"
> "最有效率的操作是找到突破點和跌破點"
> "只有量跟價，其他的技術指標，像是 MACD、RSI、KD 指標，全部不用看"

## The 12 Patterns (12 神招)

### Priority Patterns (Highest Success Rate)

#### 1. 破底翻 (Spring / Bottom Reversal) ⭐⭐⭐
**Success Rate: 75-80%**

- Price breaks below established support
- Quickly reverses and closes back above support
- Looks like a "bear trap"
- **Volume**: Breakdown on moderate volume, reversal on VERY heavy volume
- **Entry**: When price closes back above support
- **Stop**: Below the spring low (3-5%)
- **Target**: Opposite side of trading range or measured move

#### 2. 假突破 (False Breakout) ⭐⭐⭐
**Success Rate: 70-75%**

Two types:
- **Bull Trap (多頭陷阱)**: Breaks above resistance on light volume, reverses on heavy volume → SELL
- **Bear Trap (空頭陷阱)**: Breaks below support on light volume, reverses on heavy volume → BUY

**Key**: Volume confirmation is critical
- Light volume on breakout = lack of conviction
- Heavy volume on reversal = smart money acting

#### 3. 突破點 (Breakout Point) ⭐⭐
**Success Rate: 70%+**

- Price breaks above resistance with volume surge
- "在突破 (頸線) 的那一剎那，價格出現，量也會推升"
- **Volume**: Must be 1.5x+ average volume
- **Entry**: On breakout with volume confirmation
- **Stop**: Below support level (5-8%)
- **Target**: Measured move (pattern height projected upward)

### Secondary Patterns

#### 4. W-bottom (雙重底)
- Two distinct lows at same level
- Second bottom may be slightly higher (bullish)
- Volume: Second bottom lighter than first, breakout heavy
- Success: ~70-75% with volume confirmation

#### 5. M-top (雙重頂)
- Two distinct peaks at same level
- Second peak usually lower (volume divergence)
- Volume: Second peak lighter than first
- Success: ~70% with volume divergence

#### 6. Head & Shoulders Bottom (頭肩底)
- Three troughs: left shoulder, head (lowest), right shoulder
- Volume: Declining from left to right, surge on breakout
- Success: ~75-80% with volume confirmation

#### 7. Head & Shoulders Top (頭肩頂)
- Three peaks: left shoulder, head (highest), right shoulder
- Volume: Declining from left to right
- Success: ~75%

#### 8. Ascending Triangle (上升三角形)
- Flat resistance, rising support
- Typically bullish continuation
- Volume: Decreases during formation, surges on breakout
- Success: ~70-75%

#### 9. Descending Triangle (下降三角形)
- Flat support, declining resistance
- Typically bearish continuation
- Volume: Decreases during formation, increases on breakdown
- Success: ~65-70%

#### 10. Bullish Flag (下飄旗形)
- Sharp rally (flagpole) on heavy volume
- Downward consolidation on light volume
- Success: ~75%

#### 11. Bearish Flag (上飄旗形)
- Sharp decline (flagpole) on heavy volume
- Upward consolidation on light volume
- Success: ~70-75%

#### 12. 問題量 (Problem Volume / Volume Divergence)
- Not a pattern, but a warning signal
- Price makes new high but volume is lower
- Use as exit signal, not entry

## Volume Analysis Framework

### Volume-Price Relationships

| Volume | Price | Interpretation | Signal |
|--------|-------|----------------|--------|
| Heavy ↑ | Up ↑ | 量價齊揚 | Bullish confirmation |
| Light ↓ | Up ↑ | 量價背離 | Bearish divergence ⚠️ |
| Heavy ↑ | Down ↓ | 量增價跌 | Bearish - heavy selling |
| Light ↓ | Down ↓ | 量縮價跌 | Selling exhaustion watch 👀 |

### Volume Thresholds

- **異常大量 (Unusually heavy)**: > 2.0x average
- **大量 (Heavy)**: > 1.5x average
- **正常 (Normal)**: 0.8-1.5x average
- **量縮 (Light)**: 0.5-0.8x average
- **極量縮 (Very light)**: < 0.5x average

### Volume Spike Analysis

When volume spikes (>2x average):
1. Note the date and price
2. Watch what happens in next 3-5 days
3. If price moves in direction of volume, confirmation
4. If price reverses, potential trap

## Trend Line Drawing Method

### 蔡森's Approach

1. **Find significant pivot points** (peaks and valleys)
2. **Connect at least 2 points** to form a line
3. **Project forward** to current price
4. **Watch for breaks** of trend lines

### Support Line (支撐線)
- Connect at least 2 significant lows
- Rising support = bullish
- Falling support = bearish
- Break below = potential sell signal

### Resistance Line (阻力線)
- Connect at least 2 significant highs
- Rising resistance = being tested
- Falling resistance = bearish
- Break above = potential buy signal

### Key Principle
"支撐不破看滿足" - If support holds, target the measured move

## Swing Analysis (波段漲跌幅)

### Measuring Swings

1. **Identify swing high and low** (past 3-6 months)
2. **Calculate current position** in the range
3. **Measure historical swing sizes** for target estimation

### Position Interpretation

| Position in Range | Interpretation |
|-------------------|----------------|
| > 80% | Near high - watch for false breakout or top pattern |
| 60-80% | Upper middle - bullish, watch for continuation |
| 40-60% | Middle - consolidation, wait for direction |
| 20-40% | Lower middle - bearish, watch for reversal |
| < 20% | Near low - watch for spring (破底翻) or bottom pattern |

### Target Calculation

**For breakouts:**
- Target = Breakout level + Pattern height
- Pattern height = Resistance - Support

**Example:**
- Support: 100, Resistance: 120
- Pattern height: 20
- Breakout at: 120
- Target: 120 + 20 = 140

## Risk Management Rules

### Position Sizing
- Never risk more than 2% of capital on single trade
- Maximum position: 25% of portfolio

### Stop-Loss Placement
- Based on pattern structure, not arbitrary %
- W-bottom: Below second bottom (5-8%)
- Spring: Below spring low (3-5%)
- Breakout: Below support level (5-8%)
- Flag: Below flag low (3-5%)

### Risk/Reward Ratio
- Minimum acceptable: 1:2
- Ideal: 1:3 or better
- Calculate BEFORE entering trade

### The "Three Hearts" (三心)
1. **耐心 (Patience)**: Wait for pattern to complete
2. **決心 (Decisiveness)**: Enter when signal appears
3. **平常心 (Equanimity)**: Don't panic on short-term fluctuations

## Practical Application

### Step-by-Step Analysis

1. **Check Volume First**
   - Current volume vs average
   - Volume trend (increasing/decreasing)
   - Volume-price relationship

2. **Identify Pattern**
   - Look for the 12 patterns
   - Priority: 破底翻，假突破，突破點
   - Confirm with volume

3. **Draw Trend Lines**
   - Connect significant pivots
   - Project to current price
   - Note distance to support/resistance

4. **Measure Swing Position**
   - Where is price in the range?
   - What's the potential upside/downside?
   - Calculate measured move targets

5. **Make Decision**
   - Buy signal: 破底翻，bullish 假突破，confirmed 突破點
   - Sell signal: bearish 假突破， breakdown from pattern
   - Hold: No clear pattern, mixed signals

### Entry Checklist
- [ ] Pattern clearly identified
- [ ] Volume confirms the pattern
- [ ] Risk/reward ratio ≥ 1:2
- [ ] Stop-loss level defined
- [ ] Target price calculated
- [ ] Position size calculated (max 2% risk)
