## Core Theory: Four-Cycle Nested Framework

Based on Schumpeter's three-cycle theory, Zhou added real estate cycle:

| Cycle | Name | Duration | Driver |
|-------|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | 康波周期 (Kondratiev) | 50-60 years | Technological innovation |
| 2 | 房地产周期 (Kuznets/Real Estate) | ~20 years | Construction/infrastructure |
| 3 | 朱格拉周期 (Juglar/Investment) | ~9 years | Fixed investment |
| 4 | 基钦周期 (Kitchin/Inventory) | ~18 months | Inventory cycles |

**Key Insight:** A person's lifetime experiences:
- 1 Kondratiev cycle
- 3 Real estate cycles
- 9 Juglar cycles
- 18 Kitchin cycles

## Zhou's Methodology Deep Dive (from 2016 Speech)

### Core Philosophy:
> "周期是经济、技术、社会系统及社会制度的综合产物，在周期大系统面前，若干个宏观经济指标的变化只是表面现象。对周期研究的最高境界是以对系统的洞察来理解细节的变化。"

(Cycles are the comprehensive product of economic, technological, social systems and institutions. In front of the cycle system, changes in macroeconomic indicators are just surface phenomena. The highest realm of cycle research is to understand detailed changes through system insight.)

### Key Methodological Principles:

1. **System-Based Analysis (结构主义):**
   - Don't equate cycle fluctuations with cyclical industry performance
   - Cycles are complex systems affecting ALL asset classes
   - Focus on system dynamics, not individual indicators

2. **Four-Cycle Nested Model:**
   - 60-year Kondratiev → 3 × 20-year Real Estate → 6 × 10-year Fixed Investment → 18 × 3-year Inventory
   - Each cycle nests within the larger one
   - Turning points occur when multiple cycles align

3. **Asset Rotation Logic:**
   - **Recession Phase (2004-2015):** Stocks, bonds profitable (easy money)
   - **Depression Phase (2015-2025):** Asset returns decline, focus on capital preservation
   - **Key Shift:** 2015 = turning point from "profit-seeking" to "capital preservation"

4. **Liquidity Preference Framework:**
   - Future 5 years = asset decline period
   - Priority 1: Capital preservation (保值)
   - Priority 2: Liquidity (流动性)
   - Avoid illiquid assets during depression phase

5. **Wealth Opportunity Identification:**
   - Opportunities come from CYCLE TIMING, not personal ability
   - Three opportunities per lifetime (aligned with Kondratiev phases)
   - 2008 (first), 2019 (second), 2030 (third)

6. **Regional Differentiation:**
   - **Dominant nations (US):** Lead technological innovation
   - **Catching-up nations (China):** Technology adoption/penetration
   - **Resource nations (Russia, Australia, Brazil):** Commodity-dependent
   - Each plays different role in cycle phases

### Prediction Framework:
1. Identify current Kondratiev phase (Prosperity/Recession/Depression/Recovery)
2. Map nested cycles (Real Estate, Fixed Investment, Inventory)
3. Determine asset class performance by phase
4. Identify turning points (typically when 2+ cycles align)
5. Adjust strategy: profit-seeking vs. capital preservation
